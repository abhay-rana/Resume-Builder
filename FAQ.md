## General Questions

### Is ResumeFreePro really free?

**Yes, 100% free.** No hidden costs, no credit card required, no premium tiers. All features are available to everyone at no cost.

### Why is it free?

We believe everyone deserves access to professional resume tools. The project is supported by the community and we keep costs low through efficient engineering.

### Do I need to create an account?

**No signup required.** You can create, edit, and download your resume without creating an account. Your data is stored locally in your browser.

### Is my data safe?

Yes. Your resume data is stored locally in your browser. We don't store your personal information on our servers. You have full control over your data.

## Features

### What file formats can I download?

Currently, we support **PDF download**. PDF ensures your resume looks exactly the same on any device or when printed.

### Can I import my existing resume?

Yes! You can:
- **Upload a PDF** - Our AI extracts all your information
- **Import from LinkedIn** - Auto-fill from your profile
- **Start fresh** - Build from scratch

### How does AI import work?

Upload your existing resume as a PDF. Our AI analyzes the document and automatically extracts:
- Personal information
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills
- Projects

You can then edit and improve the extracted content.

### Are the templates ATS-friendly?

**Yes, all templates are ATS-optimized.** They're designed to:
- Use standard section headers
- Maintain clean formatting
- Parse correctly by all major ATS systems
- Pass automated screening

### Can I customize the design?

Yes, extensive customization options include:
- **Colors** - Primary, secondary, accent colors
- **Fonts** - 20+ professional fonts
- **Spacing** - Margins, line height, section spacing
- **Icons** - Enable/disable icons
- **Layout** - Section ordering, visibility

### Is there a page limit?

No limit. Create single-page or multi-page resumes based on your needs. Our preview shows exact page breaks so you know how your resume will print.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

ResumeFreePro works on all modern browsers:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### Does it work on mobile?

Yes, but we recommend using a desktop/laptop for the best experience. The resume editor is optimized for larger screens.

### Why isn't my PDF downloading?

Try these steps:
1. Disable ad blockers temporarily
2. Allow pop-ups for our site
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache

### My imported resume looks wrong

AI parsing isn't perfect. After import:
1. Review all sections
2. Fix any parsing errors
3. Add missing information
4. Adjust formatting

## Resume Advice

### How long should my resume be?

| Experience | Length |
|------------|--------|
| Entry level (0-2 years) | 1 page |
| Mid-level (3-7 years) | 1-2 pages |
| Senior (8+ years) | 2 pages |

See our [Resume Length Guide](guides/resume-length.md) for details.

### What should I include?

**Essential sections:**
- Contact information
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills

**Optional sections:**
- Projects
- Certifications
- Awards
- Volunteer work
- Languages

### How do I beat ATS systems?

1. Use standard section headers
2. Include keywords from job description
3. Avoid tables and graphics
4. Use standard fonts
5. Submit as PDF

See our [ATS Optimization Guide](guides/ats-optimization.md) for complete details.

### Should I include a photo?

**In the US/UK:** No, don't include a photo.
**In Europe/Asia:** Sometimes expected. Check local norms.

### What about references?

Don't include "References available upon request." It's assumed and wastes space. Prepare references separately.
